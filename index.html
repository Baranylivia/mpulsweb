About the Research Group

Róbert Szabó's second Lendület project (2025-2030) 'Challenges from astronomical data explosion: questions and answers from multi-D astrophysical modelling' is about numerical modelling of classical pulsating variable stars (RR Lyrae stars and Cepheids). In the course of the project we are committed to develop a new, modular, user-friendly numerical code with improved physics.
